Personal Finance Analysis

A non-scientific, statistically-insignificant analysis and visualization of my personal spending. Data also utilized to create web API and ML bot.

Some interesting observations:

  • My spending behaviour is dependent on location (Figure 1.)
  • My eating behaviour is dependent on day of week (Figure 2.)
  • My most frequented stores are Superstore and McDonald's (Figure 3.)
  • Housing and transportation is the only spending category that is projected to increase over time (Figure 4.)
